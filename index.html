Bluetreehost Has Ceased Business Operations

Bluetreehost has closed its doors and is no longer providing hosting services as of December 31st, 2023.

We launched Bluetreehost in 2007 with the goal of providing affordable and reliable hosting services to our customers. We are grateful for the support we received from our customers over the years. Given the hosting landscape and shifts over the years, we decided to cease operations and focus on other ventures. We appreciate your understanding and support over the years! Onward and upward.
